Biography

"Life is hard but it fair to me tough titty but the milk is free, they ask me how I'm doing said I can't complain if I did they probably wouldn't listen anyway."

Elevating words on life from Missin' Link. Originally from the distressed streets of Cleveland, Ohio Missin' Link belts out seductive, provocative, and inspirational tunes influenced by R&B, Funk, and Hip Hop to rock your body. The boldness of his music reveals what crosses everyone's mind but only a few dare to say.

Starting from humble beginnings as student at Central State University, Missin' Link and a few friends were free styling in his kitchen at Maple View apartments in Fairborn, Ohio. Due to numerous compliments and requests for more music, he continued to write and record. As a result, he created Hell's Kitchen Entertainment. In efforts, to make himself and his team more professional and marketable he dropped the "Hell's" off because he felt it was a "negative connotation". Thus, The Kitchen Entertainment was born.

Having been in the music business a little over ten years, Missin' Link continues to remain a breath of fresh air. His live shows have been reviewed as "Tantalizing" and "Old school smooth with a new school kick". Ultimately, if you have never had the "Missin Link Experience" stop half stepping and "GET THERE!"
